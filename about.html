Prof. Mahesh Panchagnula

Profile

Prof. Mahesh Panchagnula

Mahesh V. Panchagnula graduated from IIT Madras in 1992 with a B. Tech in Mechanical Engineering. He subsequently obtained an MSME in 1994 and Ph.D. in ME in 1997, both from Purdue University. He has since worked in industrial research positions with Goodrich Aerospace and Excel India till 2004. He has served as a Research Fellow at Lehigh University and as an Assistant Professor of Mechanical Engineering at Tennessee Technological University from 2005 to 2010. He has been on the faculty of the Department of Applied Mechanics, Indian Institute of Technology Madras since 2010 and in the current rank since 2014.

He is also the co-founder of the IIT Madras pre-incubator Nirmaan and the associate faculty-in-charge of the IIT Madras Incubation Cell, which advises faculty and students entrepreneurship activities.

Dr. Panchagnula’s current research broadly focuses on fundamental interdisciplinary multiphase thermal/ fluid phenomena including wetting, hysteresis in natural systems, spray formation physics,pulmonary aerosol transport, liquid-liquid wetting and spray combustion. His lab employs both experimental as well as theoretical inquiry techniques. He has worked on the problem of contactangle hysteresis and shown that it is an effect originating at the contact line. In addition, he has worked on wetting in partially miscible liquid-liquid systems such as oil/water thin film dynamics with Marangoni effects. His recent research has focused on applying simulation and data analytics tools to fluid mechanics problems such as the behaviour of large crowds and turbulence. His complete list of published work can be found on Google Scholar.

Prof. Ashwin Mahalingam

Profile

Prof. Ashwin Mahalingam

Dr. Ashwin Mahalingam joined the faculty at IIT-Madras in 2006. Prior to IIT-Madras he helped start-up a company in Silicon Valley and ran a Social-Entrepreneurship Business plan competition at Stanford University.

At IIT-Madras he is the faculty advisor of the Entrepreneurship Cell, sits on the board of the IIT-Madras Incubation Cell and co-founded IITM's pre-incubation program - Nirmaan. He is also a co-Founder and Director of Okapi Advisory Services Pvt Ltd.

Prof.Satyanarayanan Seshadri

Profile

Prof.Satyanarayanan Seshadri

Prof. Satyanarayanan is the faculty advisor for Nirmaan. He is currently a faculty member in the department of Applied Mechanics. His lab, Energy and Emissions focuses advancing science and technology in the domains of energy storage, waste heat recovery and emission control.

He obtained his PhD in Mechanical Engineering, with specialization in Aerosol Science from Texas A&M University, College Station in 2007 and his postdoctoral research experience at Rutgers University, New Jersey in the field of bioaerosol quantification and electrostatic methods for emission control. Prior to his current role at IITM, he worked on various capacities at GE’s Global Research Center in Bangalore and headed the Chennai R&D center for steam engineering and instrumentation company Forbes Marshall.

On the policy side, Dr. Satya coordinates the energy group at the Centre for Technology and Policy (CTaP) at IIT Madras where they work on technology, policy and economic aspects of industrial energy use. He is also a member of the energy sub-committee at South Indian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (SICCI), trying to identify and remove bottlenecks in clean energy technology transition to industry.

Dr. Hiran Vedam

Profile

Dr. Hiran Vedam

Dr. Hiran Vedam is a business executive with more than 17 years of experience in commercialization of high quality technologies. Her domain expertise is in advanced materials and their applications for energy and water. Functionally, her expertise is in product development and scaleup, competitive technology and market intelligence and B2B market development.

She is currently MD at nH2 – an international company that commercializes breakthroughs in advanced material technologies from world-class universities.

She was an Advisor for IP & Entrepreneurship at IIT Gandhinagar, where she started the innovation and entrepreneurship program from scratch in a 6 year old educational institution and helped the Institute secure DST-TBI funding for setting up an incubator.

She founded ArdeJa Pte.Ltd. (previously known as NanoConsulting Pte. Ltd.) in Singapore which offered consulting services to advanced material startups and government bodies. Prior to founding ArdeJa, she was a Senior Manager at Industry Liaison Office at National University of Singapore, where she was responsible for commercialization of advanced material technologies. Earlier in her career, she was the founder of Matrika Pte. Ltd. and the first employee and Principal Scientist at Honeywell R&D Center - Singapore.

Hiran has a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from Purdue University, MBA from INSEAD, M.S. from Washington University and a B.Tech from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras.

J. Jeyappriadhevi

Profile

J. Jeyappriadhevi

Mrs. J. Jeyappriadhevi is the Founder and Managing Director of Kriyative Learning solution international private limited, an educational innovation and research organization. She is an eminent authority on multiple intelligences in primary and preprimary curriculum for schools.

She is also the author of the KRIYA learning Framework, which integrates learning methodology with multiple intelligences, thereby giving a strong background for a holistic educational program. Her recent research focuses on multiple intelligences based criteria and evaluation of a student, which transcends several assumptions and goes into giving a significant meaning to the likes and dislikes of an individual. Her specialty is applying all these principles in school education scenario.

Jeyappria has completed her bachelor of engineering from Madras University, and later pursued Master of business administration from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Chennai. She is a certified Green belt in six sigma from KPMG, and several other quality programs from quality council of India.

She is a distinguished alumni of Department of management studies, IIT-Madras. Currently serves as a treasurer of IIT madras Alumni Association. She is also a mentor in Healthcare Technology Incubation Center, IIT MRP, and Core-Committee member of NIRMAAN, a pre-incubation center at IIT M.
