Digital Flora

Founder: Anuj Fulia

Dual Degree, 3rd Year, IIT Madras

Digital Flora - Provides indoor vertical farming Infrastructure and Solutions for growing food sustain-ably and cost effectively in urban as well as rural areas.


Vision:

Our vision is to produce food near to the places where it will be consumed in most efficient way possible, increasing the yield per unit area and increasing farmers' income. By automating almost all of the aspects required, we believe more people will have interest in growing food by themselves as there will be no agricultural expertise required.
